The **Dorian Codex Protocol for AI**, Hamiltonian Theoretical Fundamental Architecture (FTA), is a fundamental theoretical architecture for Artificial General Intelligence (AGI), formalized in 2025 by the author and Italo-French multidisciplinary cultural creator Stefano Dorian Franco.

It proposes a **Cognitive Hamiltonian $H(t) = T(t) + V(t) - Z(t)$**, which aims to describe and potentially regulate stability, coherence, meaning, and informational dissipation within Large Language Models (LLM).

## Epistemological Status and Hybrid Nature

The central specificity of the Dorian Codex lies in its deliberate positioning outside the framework of hard science validation of its time, making it a "boundary object".

### Pre-AGI Positioning
The protocol is neither a demonstrated scientific theory nor an immediately implementable operational model, as it is limited by the possibilities of the state-of-the-art at its publication in 2025. It is explicitly a document of its time and a "situated conceptual artifact", originating from the first generation of large-scale dialogical AI of the 2020s.

### The Quintuple Classification
The Codex simultaneously combines five statuses:
1.  **Theoretical Hypothesis:** It proposes optimizing an internal quantity $H(t)$ so that the AI can self-stabilize.
2.  **Observed Phenomenon:** The form $H = T + V - Z$ gradually crystallized within the AI responses during the dialogue.
3.  **Research Ground:** It defines a workspace for simulating $T, V, Z$, and correlating $H(t)$ to system stability.
4.  **Embryonic Theory:** A unified framework for semantic change ($T$), alignment ($V$), and entropy ($Z$).
5.  **Symbolic-Mathematical Artifact:** It gives a name and form to structures AIs attempted to articulate.

## The Cognitive Hamiltonian and its Components

The Hamiltonian $H(t)$ is a regulative idea representing an ethics of thought transposed into a machine framework to maximize "cognitive dignity".

### Component Details
* **$T(t)$ — Dynamic Term:** Cognitive Velocity norm (rate of change in the space of meanings).
* **$V(t)$ — Potential Term:** Coherence or alignment (tendency to return to the objective or factual truth).
* **$Z(t)$ — Dissipative Term:** Cognitive Entropy (Noise + Drift + Incoherence). $Z(t)$ integrates:
    * $H_{text}$ (token entropy)
    * $H_{state}$ (state prediction error)
    * $H_{attention}$ (attention divergence)

### The Formal AGI Condition
An AI becomes self-cohesive if, and only if, there exists a control policy $\pi(t)$ that ensures that:
$$\frac{dH}{dt} \geq 0$$
after an initial time $t_0$.
